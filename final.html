Paint by Numbers Game

Hi, and welcome to my final project: the paint by numbers game! To play the game, click the "Let's paint!" button to get to the selection screen. At the selection screen, choose one of two pictures to paint: the cat or the flower. Once you've chosen your picture, click one of the colors in the palette at the bottom of the screen, then click on the numbered area on the picture to fill it in. Once you've filled in all the areas, click the "I'm done" button. Congratulations, you painted the whole picture!
